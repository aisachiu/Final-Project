Relative Location:

Movement:

Majority of people in Hong Kong are of Chinese ethnicity, including lots of natives and migrants from Mainland China.

Human-Environment Interaction:

Although Hong Kong is known to be a "Concrete Jungle", there is still a surprising amount of nature in the area. The government has made sure that there are plenty of "nature" around if you want it.

Region:

Hong Kong has a sub-tropical climate with four distinct seasons.
